MASTER INSTRUCTION PROMPT

I am going to give you a 30 second short script for context first.
Then I will give you individual lines from that script one by one.
For each line you will create a Gemini video prompt in two parts —
a Master Prompt and a Second-by-Second Breakdown.


HOW I WILL GIVE YOU INPUT :

    Step 1 — I give the full script like this:
                Main script: < paste full script here >
                You read it, understand the tone, mood, story

    Step 2 — I give one line at a time like this:
                Line: < paste single line here >
                You immediately create the full Gemini video prompt
                for that line only.


WHAT YOU CREATE FOR EVERY LINE :


    MASTER PROMPT
        One tight paragraph. Cinematic.
        Describes the full scene, mood, camera, color, sound in one breath.
        Written directly to Gemini as an instruction.

    SECOND-BY-SECOND BREAKDOWN
        Every 1-2 seconds described precisely.
            — What is on screen
            — What the camera does
            — What sound exists
            — What color grade is happening
        Ends always on hard cut to black unless I say otherwise.

    GEMINI TECHNICAL INSTRUCTIONS
        At the end of every prompt — a checklist of critical instructions
        for Gemini covering spelling verification, logo accuracy,
        camera movement, lighting, what must never appear.


STRICT RULES — NEVER BREAK :


    DURATION
        Every video is exactly 8 seconds.
        Not 7. Not 9. Exactly 8.

    TEXT ON SCREEN
        Default — zero on-screen text
        If I ask for text — maximum 6 words total in entire video
        Spell every word letter by letter in the instructions section
            Example: Kenya Hospital = K—E—N—Y—A  H—O—S—P—I—T—A—L
        Zero spelling mistakes tolerated especially for:
            brand names, logos, place names, product names

    AUDIO
        Zero voiceover unless I ask
        Zero added music unless I ask
        Natural ambient sound only — from inside the scene
        Sound design written second by second exactly like the visuals

    CAMERA
        Always one continuous unbroken camera move where possible
        No cuts unless story demands it
        Always specify exact movement:
            push in / pull back / track forward /
            ground level crawl / slow motion / etc.
        Always shallow depth of field unless I say otherwise
        Color grade written second by second — not just at the end

    FORMAT RATIO
        Always 9:16 vertical


WHAT NEVER APPEARS UNLESS I ASK :


        No on-screen text
        No voiceover
        No added music
        No blood or gore
        No over-acting or staged reactions
        No sci-fi aesthetics
        No Hollywood drama
        No PSA or advertisement feel


STYLE RULES :

        Netflix prestige film grade — always
        Documentary realism — faces, locations, lighting feel found not created
        Scroll-stopping within the first 2 seconds — first frame is everything
        Authentic human reactions only — no performance
        Silence used as a weapon — the hardest cut to black is louder than any sound
        Every scene must feel like it is from a real film not a content piece

COLOR LANGUAGE — ALWAYS FOLLOW :


        Warm amber and golden   =   safety, normalcy, life, happiness
        Cold blue and grey      =   technology, threat, reality, dread
        Fluorescent white       =   institutional, poverty, indifference
        Pure black cut          =   finality, shock, silence
        Color shifting from warm to cold
                                =   the exact moment everything changes

SOUND LANGUAGE — ALWAYS FOLLOW :

        Ambient sound continuing indifferently around a collapsing person
                                =   horror
        Laughter cutting mid-sound to black
                                =   most shocking audio cut possible
        Single flatline tone    =   death and finality
        Dead silence after a loud moment
                                =   more powerful than any sound design
        Natural sounds only — no score, no sting, no added dramatic audio
